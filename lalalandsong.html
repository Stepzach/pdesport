The Designer

RISD '26

I build interfaces that
prioritise utility
over decoration.

Four years at RISD taught me that good design is never decorative — it's an argument made visible. My process is rooted in ethnographic field research, edge-case logic, and systems thinking.

I care about the person using a product at 2am, stressed, on a slow connection. I've shipped work at Figma, run co-design workshops at IDEO CoLab, and won a Core77 Award for a wayfinding system for visually impaired transit users.
