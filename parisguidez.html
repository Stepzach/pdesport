Le Petit Chêne

french Neo-Bistro 2eme Arr.

tucked away down a quiet alley in the 11th arrondissement, Le Petit Chêne redefines classic French comfort food. The seasonal menu relies entirely on hyper-local farm produce, served in an intimate, candle-lit dining room that feels like stepping into a private dinner party. Don't skip the comté gougères.
