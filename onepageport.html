✦ About Me

I design with
intention.

I'm a product designer who grew up watching her parents navigate incomprehensible government forms and banking apps — so now I make things that actually make sense to real people, not just people who already know how it works.

My work lives at the overlap of rigorous research, systems thinking, and the kind of craft that makes a button feel exactly right. I believe constraints are a gift, white space is a decision, and shipping is a skill.

When I'm not in Figma: bouldering, reading about cognitive psychology, and aggressively recommending books to people who didn't ask.

✦ How I Work

The process
behind the
pixels.

01

Understand
before you build.

Research, competitive analysis, stakeholder alignment. I don't touch Figma until I understand the actual problem.

02

Explore
widely & wildly.

Sketches. Crazy ideas. 10 directions before committing to one. I push the brief before honoring it.

03

Test
early, test often.

Paper prototypes to high-fidelity — I validate assumptions with real users at every stage of fidelity.

04

Ship
and iterate.

Design is never done, just released. I stay close after launch and treat feedback as fuel.
